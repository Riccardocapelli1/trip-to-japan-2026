# Spec: Journey Overview

## Goal
Provide the traveler and their companion with a quick, high-level visualization of the entire 15-day itinerary to understand the trip's flow and energy requirements.

## User Persona
- **Ricky**: The primary planner who wants to ensure the trip is coherent.
- **Companion**: Wants to visualize the adventure and understand the travel pace.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] The full timeline from Oct 31 to Nov 15 is visible.
- [ ] City transitions (e.g., Tokyo -> Takayama) are clearly demarcated.
- [ ] Each day displays an "Intensity" level (Low, Medium, High).

## Detailed Flow
1. **Entry**: User lands on the home page.
2. **Engagement**: User scrolls past the hero section to the "Japón 2026" timeline.
3. **Scan**: User observes the sequential order of cities and dates.
4. **Assessment**: User identifies high-intensity days (e.g., travel days to Nagoya/Takayama) to prepare mentally.
